Healthy Tips

Hydration
Drink water regularly

Start the day with a glass of water and keep a bottle nearby. Flavor with lemon or mint if needed.

Protein at every meal
Supports muscle & satiety

Include beans, yogurt, eggs, fish, or lean meat to improve fullness and recovery.

Colorful plates
Eat the rainbow

Variety of colors usually means a wider range of vitamins and antioxidants.
